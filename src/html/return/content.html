RETURN/EXCHANGE PROCEDURE

1. Download (DOWNLOAD HERE) a sample return form. Fill it out digitally or print it, fill it out by hand, and scan or photograph it. 2. Fill out the form below, providing all the necessary details. Be sure to attach the completed return form in the “Return form file” field. 3. After submitting the form, wait 24 hours for a tracking number to appear in your SDEC app, which you can use to drop off your package at your nearest pickup point.
